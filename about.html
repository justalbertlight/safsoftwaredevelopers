ABOUT US

SAF POLYTECHNIC


SAF Polytechnic is an institution that inspires passion for knowledge, skills acquisition and character development for imaginative and innovative products development for societal, moral and economic growth.


Her mission is to provide moral and high quality technological and entrepreneural educationto promote societal development.


SAF Polytechnic is aimed to provide for students training skills in Applied SCience, Engineering and BUsiness studies with a view of meeting the present and future needs of the country in commerce, industry and the public service as well as career and personal needs of students. Also to provide qualitative education for students to enable them contribute to the technological, social and economic development of the country.

COMPUTER SCIENCE DEPARTMENT


Computer Science department is one of the departments in SAF Polytechnic Iseyin. The objective of the department is to raise students who will be able to operate computer systems, use various computer packages e.g MS Word, MS Excel, CorelDraw etc, maintain hardware and solve simple hardware problems, use various programming languages: Visual BASIC (VB), JAVA, C programming, Unified Modeling Language (UML), HyperText Mark-up Language (HTML) , - use internet and set up network, set up and manage and enterprise.

SOFTWARE DEVELOPERS TEAM


Software Developers Group is a group of students who are zealous and passionate about the web technology, They are potential software engineers who are driven with passion to build the web

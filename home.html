Meet Our Core Departmental lecturers


Mr. Ganiyu Azeez (HOD)

Mr. Ganiyu Azeez Olayemi is a native of Ogbomosho, Oyo State, Nigeria. He is a B.Tech and M.Tech degree holder in Computer Engineering, He graduated from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology, Ogbomosho. His goal for Computer Science students is to raise students who can confidently develop software and to raise modelers.

Mr. Alabi Muideen (Ishow)

Mr. Alabi Muideen Adeniran Ishola popularly known as iShow is a native of Iseyin, Oyo State. Mr Ishola obtained his OND certificate from Osun State College of Technology and later bagged his HND certificate at Lagos State Polytechnic. He also has several other profesional courses certifications such as Database Management Administration. He is also a ICT expert and a lecturer in SAF Polytechnic. His goal for Computer Science students is to raise a set of students who will make computer technology be at a peak and he believes in the statement "No Technology, No World"

Mr. Ridwan

Mr. Fasasi Ridwan Segun is a graduate of Ladoke Akintola University of Technology, Ogbomosho in the department of Computer Engineering. Mr Ridwan had a B.Tech certification in Computer Engineering. He also has a certicate in Data Analysis with Microsoft. He is currently doing his Masters in LAUTECH. His areas of speciality are Programming, Data Analysis, Embedded Systems, Networking etc. He is a lecturer in SAF Polytechnic and is the current HOD of Computer Engineering Department. His goal for Computer Science students is to expose students to the knowledge of programming
